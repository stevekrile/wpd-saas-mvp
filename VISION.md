# Whole Design Suite — Long-Term Vision

## The Core Idea

Whole Process Design (WPD) is the kernel. Everything else is an expansion of the same diagnostic and design philosophy applied at a wider organizational scope.

| Layer | Core Audience | Unit of Design | Central Problem | Product Promise |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Whole Process Design** | Middle managers / process owners | A process or line of work | "This thing does not work, and I do not control all the pieces." | Diagnose and redesign a process using the four lenses. |
| **Whole Function Design** | Functional leaders | A business function | "My function is expected to deliver value, but its model, people, systems, and measures are misaligned." | Turn a function into a coherent service-delivery engine. |
| **Whole Business Design** | C-suite / enterprise leaders | The enterprise operating model | "The company strategy is clear enough, but the organization cannot execute it coherently." | Align strategy, functions, systems, governance, talent, and outcomes. |

Each layer reuses the same fundamental lens-based diagnostic approach pioneered in WPD, extended for its broader unit of design.

---

## Roadmap Phases

### Phase 1 — Whole Process Design (Current MVP)
Build the first usable version of the WPD SaaS application. Establish the core methodology, guided diagnostic, and four-lens framework as the foundation of the platform.

**Goal:** Prove the diagnostic model works as a software-guided experience for process owners and middle managers.

### Phase 2 — Whole Function Design
Expand the platform to address functional leaders. Introduce diagnostics and design tools scoped to an entire business function — not just a single process within it.

**Goal:** Help functional leaders diagnose and redesign the operating model of their function as a coherent service-delivery system.

### Phase 3 — Whole Business Design
Expand the platform to the enterprise level. Address C-suite and senior leadership teams who need to align strategy, functions, operating structures, governance, talent, and outcomes.

**Goal:** Give enterprise leaders a structured framework to diagnose and redesign the organization's ability to execute its strategy.

---

## Design Principles for the Suite

- **The kernel is the process.** Every higher-level design concept decomposes into processes. WPD thinking remains the foundation regardless of scope.
- **Same philosophy, wider aperture.** Each tier applies the same lens-based diagnostic logic to a larger unit of design — not a different methodology.
- **Platform continuity.** Users and organizations move upward through the tiers as their maturity and scope grows. The platform architecture supports this progression.
- **IP protection.** Each product surface guides structured thinking without reproducing the full intellectual property of the underlying methodology or book.
